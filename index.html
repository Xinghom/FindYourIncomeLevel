Find Your Income Level in the US

  • To satisfy people's curiosity about what their income levels are like in such a wealthy country.
  • You may hear people around you complain that they just feel poor. This visualization would help them see where their individual incomes sit exactly. (Help them feel better and be more positive with their life)
  • Most importantly, to wake up the user's sense of responsibility and tell them: You actually have the financial ability to help other people who really live in a struggle, such as Liberian and Zimbabwean.
